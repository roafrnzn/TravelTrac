Locations

Calle Crisologo

Vigan

Calle Crisologo
Vigan

Step back in time as you stroll along the cobblestone streets of Calle Crisologo in Vigan, Philippines. This UNESCO World Heritage site transports you to the colonial era with its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture. Admire the charming ancestral houses adorned with capiz shell windows, wooden balconies, and antique cobblestones. The street is lined with quaint shops offering local crafts and delicacies. As you wander through the narrow alleys, you'll feel the rich history and cultural heritage of Vigan come to life.

Ifugao

Banaue Rice Terraces

Banaue Rice Terraces
Ifugao

Marvel at the breathtaking landscape of the Banaue Rice Terraces in Ifugao, a masterpiece of ancient engineering and a testament to the ingenuity of the indigenous people. Carved into the mountain slopes, these terraces have been tending to rice crops for over 2,000 years. The emerald-green terraces create a mesmerizing mosaic that blends seamlessly with the surrounding mountains. Visitors can trek along the terraces, interacting with the local Ifugao people, and gaining a deep appreciation for their agricultural traditions.

Bohol

Chocolate Hills

Chocolate Hills
Bohol

Discover the enchanting Chocolate Hills of Bohol, a geological wonder that paints the landscape with rolling hills as far as the eye can see. During the dry season, the hills turn a rich shade of brown, resembling delectable chocolate kisses, hence the name. A visit to the Chocolate Hills Complex provides panoramic views from the observation deck, allowing you to take in the surreal beauty of these natural wonders. This unique landscape promises a delightful and memorable experience for nature enthusiasts.

Manila

Intramuros

Intramuros
Manila

Immerse yourself in the historical heart of Manila as you explore Intramuros, the walled city that encapsulates centuries of Philippine history. Walk along cobbled streets and visit iconic landmarks such as Fort Santiago, Manila Cathedral, and San Agustin Church. The architecture reflects a mix of Spanish and Filipino influences, offering a glimpse into the country's colonial past. Intramuros is not just a physical space but a journey through time, making it a must-visit destination for history buffs and culture seekers.

Zambales

Mapanuepe Lake

Mapanuepe Lake
Zambales

Discover the tranquility of Mapanuepe Lake in Zambales, a hidden oasis nestled amidst rolling hills. Immerse yourself in the serene ambiance by strolling along the lakeside or engaging in peaceful boating activities. Mapanuepe Lake offers a brief escape into nature, providing a calm retreat for those seeking respite from the demands of urban life. Enjoy the unspoiled beauty and pristine environment that make this lake a perfect destination for relaxation and restoration.

Cebu

Kawasan Falls

Kawasan Falls
Cebu

Embark on a journey to the mesmerizing Kawasan Falls in Cebu, where turquoise waters cascade down lush greenery, creating a picturesque oasis. The multi-tiered waterfalls offer a refreshing escape, inviting visitors to take a dip in the cool, clear pools. Adventurous souls can engage in canyoneering, adding an adrenaline rush to the natural beauty. The surrounding tropical scenery and the rhythmic sound of cascading water make Kawasan Falls a haven for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

Siargao

Cloud 9

Cloud 9
Siargao

Surfer's paradise awaits at Cloud 9 in Siargao, a renowned surf break that attracts wave riders from around the globe. Feel the adrenaline as you ride the powerful waves of the Pacific Ocean. Beyond surfing, the laid-back atmosphere of Siargao offers a perfect blend of beach vibes and island tranquility. Enjoy breathtaking sunsets, explore hidden lagoons, and embrace the easygoing lifestyle of this surfing haven. Discover vibrant coral reefs through snorkeling and diving adventures.

Boracay

White Beach

White Beach
Boracay

Bask in the beauty of White Beach in Boracay, consistently hailed as one of the world's best beaches. Powder-fine white sand meets crystal-clear azure waters, creating a postcard-perfect tropical paradise. Whether you're into water sports, beach parties, or simply unwinding under the swaying palm trees, White Beach caters to every beachgoer's dream. The vibrant nightlife and diverse dining options complete the experience, making Boracay a must-visit destination for sun-seekers and party enthusiasts alike.
